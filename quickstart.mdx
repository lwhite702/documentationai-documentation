## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for advanced integrations
</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">
No credit card or downloads required to get started.
</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Sign up for Klutr in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Klutr" icon="globe">
    Go to [https://klutr.com/signup](https://klutr.com/signup) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user-plus">
    Provide your email, name, and a secure password. Agree to the terms.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Klutr. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to [https://app.klutr.com](https://app.klutr.com) and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, create a space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**. Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and select a starter template.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Settings" icon="settings">
    Choose public or private visibility. Add a description and custom domain if needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish Space" icon="globe">
    Click **Create**. Your space is now live at `https://app.klutr.com/spaces/my-project-docs`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Organize Initial Documents

Populate your space with content.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Create New Doc" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document**. Use the MDX editor to add content.

````mdx
## Welcome

This is your first document.

<Callout kind="success">
Space created successfully!
</Callout>
````

    Save and publish.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Upload Files" icon="upload">
    Drag and drop Markdown or MDX files into the space root. Klutr auto-organizes them.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Import from GitHub" icon="github">
    Connect your repo via **Integrations > GitHub**. Sync docs automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
