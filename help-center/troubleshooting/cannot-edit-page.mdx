---
title: Cannot Edit a Documentation Page
description: Solve issues with editing pages in Klutr.
---

## Symptoms

- Edit button is greyed out
- Changes are not saving

## Causes

- Lack of permissions
- Page locked by another editor

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ask your project admin to verify your edit access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock the page">
    If locked, wait a few minutes or contact the current editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>
