---
title: Adding a Team Member
description: Invite colleagues to collaborate in Klutr.
---

## Overview

Share your Klutr project with teammates for collaboration.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open project settings">
    Go to the desired project and click **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite user">
    Enter the teammate's email address in **Invite Member**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invitation">
    Click **Send Invite**. The user will receive an email to join your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
